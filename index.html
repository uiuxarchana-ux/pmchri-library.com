Welcome To

PMCHRI CENTRAL LIBRARY

OPAC (AutoLib)

The PMCHRI CENTRAL LIBRARY is well equipped with medical books, CDs and DVDs, bound volumes, and national and international journals. The library has a carpet area of 3235 square meters and is spread over two floors with separate reading halls for students and staff members. The centrally air-conditioned and automated library complements the rich learning experience. A separate digital library with 75 computers and a dedicated high-speed internet connection is made available on the library premises.

The library has subscribed to 60 national and international journals (print) and also subscribed to 4559 e-journals and 17928 e-books (ProQuest) every year.

The central library has 11,924 books from various departments.

We have many facilities that are designed to give you the ability to choose from different learning areas, such as the reading hall, stockroom, video/DVD room, journal section, conference hall, digital and virtual library, and outside reading hall.

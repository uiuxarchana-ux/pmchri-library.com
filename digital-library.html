DIGITAL LIBRARY

A digital library is a special library with a focused collection of digital objects that can include text, visual material, audio material, and video material stored as electronic media formats, along with means for organizing, storing, and retrieving the files and media contained in the library collection. Digital libraries can vary immensely in size and scope, and can be maintained by individuals or organizations, or be affiliated with established physical library buildings or institutions, or with academic institutions. The electronic content may be stored locally or accessed remotely via computer networks. An electronic library is a type of information retrieval system.

DIGITAL LIBRARY RULES AND REGULATIONS

  • The digital library of Panimalar Medical College Hospital and Research Institute has a good collection of e-books and e-journals for its members. The library provides internet browsing and e-services for its users.
  • Online chatting, playing games, and browsing for social networking sites are strictly prohibited.
  • Changing the settings or display of the computers in the library is not allowed. Readers are instructed not to remove or unplug computer cable connections, network cables, and other peripherals/accessories in the library.
  • The electronic resources available in the PMCHRI library are governed by license agreements. Each user is responsible for ensuring that he/she uses these products only for non-commercial, educational, scholarly, or research use without systematically downloading and distributing portions of information.
  • Users are advised to make use of these facilities only to support their academic and research activities.
  • The chief librarian is provided with the right to stop extending this facility to a person found misusing it, apart from initiating action as per the rules provided by the PMCHRI authorities.
  • The library provides printing facilities to its readers on all working days. If the purpose of printing is found to be personal work, Rs. 2/- per page will be charged. (Please send the documents to the following email ID: pmchrilibrary@gmail.com )
